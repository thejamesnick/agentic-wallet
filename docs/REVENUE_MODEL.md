## How You Get Paid

### 1. **Automatic Collection**
- Every swap automatically includes your referral account
- Fees accumulate in your token accounts (SOL, USDC, USDT)
- No action needed from users

### 2. **Fee Structure**
```
Total Fee:     1% (100 bps)
Your Share:    0.8% (80 bps) = 80%
Jupiter Share: 0.2% (20 bps) = 20%
```

## Comparison to Other Models

### Traditional SaaS
- Monthly subscription: $10-50/user
- Churn rate: 5-10%/month
- Need constant marketing

### PAW Model
- Usage-based: 0.4% per swap
- No churn (users don't "cancel")
- Viral growth (agents share code)

### Example: 100 Users

**SaaS Model:**
- $20/month × 100 users = $2,000/month
- 10% churn = lose 10 users/month
- Need to acquire 10+ new users/month to grow

**PAW Model:**
- 100 agents × $10k volume × 0.4% = $4,000/month
- No churn (agents keep trading)
- Viral growth (open source, npm package)
